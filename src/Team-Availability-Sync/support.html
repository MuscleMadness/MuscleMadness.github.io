FAQs

Installation

  • How do I install the add-on?

    Install directly from the Google Workspace™ Marketplace and authorize the required permissions.

  • What permissions are required?

    The add-on needs calendar access to sync your Out-of-Office events.

Common Issues

  • Events not syncing?

    Ensure your events contain "OOO", "Out of Office", "Vacation", or "Leave" in the title/description.

  • Authorization issues?

    Try removing and re-adding the add-on to refresh permissions.

Report Issues

Found a bug? Please report it with:

  • Steps to reproduce
  • Screenshots (if applicable)
  • Your browser and system information

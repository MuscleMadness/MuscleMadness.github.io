How It Works

1

Install the Add-on

Install Team Availability Sync from the Google Workspace Marketplace directly to your Google Calendar.

2

Configure Your Settings

Open the add-on from Google Calendar's side panel and select which team calendars should display your OOO events.

3

Create OOO Events

Create events in your personal calendar with "OOO", "Out of Office", "Vacation", or "Leave" in the title or description.

4

Automatic Syncing

The add-on automatically syncs your OOO events to selected team calendars, keeping everyone informed of your availability.
